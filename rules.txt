The Core Scoring Logic
Basic Scores: Points progress from 0 to 3 using the terms: "Love", "15", "30", and "40".
All Scores: If both players have the same score and it is below 3 points, the score is appended with "-All" (e.g., "Love-All", "15-All", "30-All").
Deuce: If both players have won at least 3 points and their scores are equal, the score is always "Deuce".
Advantage: If both players have won at least 3 points and one player has 1 more point than the other, the score is "Advantage player1" or "Advantage player2".
Win: A game is won by the first player to have won at least 4 points total and have at least 2 points more than their opponent (e.g., "Win for player1" or "Win for player2").
